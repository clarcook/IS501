About Public Administration

Public Administration is for those who have the desire to make an impact; individuals are passionated about service and finding inovative solutions to complex, challenging problems. Here are a few reasons why I chose to study Public Admin:

  1. Leadership Skills: Interested in managing teams and organizations.
  2. Understanding Program Impact: Curious about how effective programs are for the population they are intended to serve.
  3. Ethics and Accountability: Interested in fairness and transparency in government.
  4. Nonprofit Experience: Background in nonprofit work and strategy.

Potential Careers

There are many career paths that a gradaute of the MPA program can choose from. Trends in MPA careers are as follows:

  • Local, State, & Federal Government
    • City Manager: Overseeing local government operations.
    • Public Policy Analyst: Analyzing and recommending policy improvements.

  • Nonprofit Management
    • Executive Director: Leading and managing nonprofit organizations.
    • Grant Manager: Managing grant writing and execution.

  • Higher Ed
    • University Administrator: Managing academic and student services.
    • Admissions Director: Overseeing student recruitment and selection.

  • Consulting
    • Public Sector Consultant: Advising government agencies on strategy and efficiency.
    • Nonprofit Consultant: Providing strategic advice to nonprofit organizations.
